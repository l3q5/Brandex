ABOUT US

About Us

Brandex was born from the desire to provide successful launches, facilitating the union between brands and influencers. For this, a technology was developed based on data processing by Artificial Intelligence.

We are much more than a simple website, we are a platform designed to boost and enhance brands through a unique search engine, which uses artificial intelligence to find the perfect influencers for each company.

How do we do it?

We want results, and for that, we need accurate moves. That's why we combine Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Big Data to develop a complete tool, capable of analyzing data and finding the influencers that best match each brand.

Who do we do it for?

If you are an entrepreneur, business owner or agency looking to launch a product/service or even boost your sales or influencer marketing strategies, you came to the right place! Brandex was developed for those who want performance and get results.

Our mission

Our mission is to boost brands and products, facilitating communication and meeting between companies and influencers. Our commitment to innovation and technology was established with the aim of offering the best solutions for those companies that want to grow. We combine Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning and Big Data to create an innovative tool that will power your influencer marketing, ensuring a successful launch.

Our values

Our mission is to leverage influencer marketing for companies to boost brands and ensure their success. For this, we have commitment, performance and results as pillars of our mission, being values that move us.
